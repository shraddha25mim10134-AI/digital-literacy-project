Social Media and Privacy :
Lock Your Profile: When you are using Facebook or Instagram you should use the Lock Profile or Private Account setting. This will stop people you do not know from taking your photos or seeing the information about you.
You should use passwords. Do not use your name or something simple like 12345 as a password. Instead use a mix of letters and numbers and symbols like the at sign or the and sign.
You should also turn on the Two-Step Login. Enable the Two-Factor Authentication on WhatsApp and Gmail. This means that even if someone knows your password, they will not be able to log in without getting a code on your phone.
Be careful when you click on links. If you get a message about a Free Recharge or a Job Offer with a link do not click on it. These are usually tricks to steal your information.
Money and UPI Safety :
Your UPI PIN is for when you are giving money. Remember you never need to enter your UPI PIN to get money. If someone asks for your PIN to send you a prize or to give you a refund they are trying to cheat you.
When you are paying at a shop or to a friend always check the name that comes up after you scan the QR code. Make sure it is the person before you pay.
Apps and Internet :
When you install an app like a photo editor check what it is asking for. If it is asking for things, it does not need like your location or your contacts do not allow it.
Do not use Wi-Fi to check your bank account or pay bills. Do not use the Wi-Fi at a railway station or a cafe, for these things. Use your mobile data instead.
